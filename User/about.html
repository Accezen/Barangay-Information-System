ABOUT BARANGAY CAPAOAY

MISSION

To be able to actively carry out the mandates and ensure transparency, honesty, and efficiency in the delivery of services in the barangay.

VISION

A progressive barangay governed by honest, sincere, hardworking, and united officials constitute with discipline, healthy, educated, cooperative, law abiding, and God-fearing people with so much oppurtunities in agriculture, industrial, and commercial aspect updated with modernization and maintain to be accessible safe, clean peaceful, and orderly place.
